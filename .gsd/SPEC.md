# SPEC.md — Project Specification

> **Status**: `FINALIZED`

## Vision
ChanakyaOS is an AI-powered career operating system designed to help students and young professionals build the right profile, roadmap, and proof of readiness required to secure internships, jobs, and long-term career success. It embodies the principle that the smartest, most prepared person wins the career game.

## Goals
1. **Career Clarity**: Reduce career confusion for students from all academic backgrounds (especially non-tech).
2. **Profile Hardening**: Identify and fill skill gaps in resumes and LinkedIn profiles.
3. **Actionable Pathways**: Generate realistic, multi-path career roadmaps tailored to user background and constraints.
4. **Measurable Outcomes**: Improve career readiness scores and drive internship/job success.

## Non-Goals (Out of Scope)
- **Direct Job Placement**: ChanakyaOS is a guidance and preparation platform, not a recruitment agency.
- **Content Creation**: The platform suggests projects but does not host the projects themselves (e.g., GitHub handles code).
- **Physical Mentorship**: Focus is on AI-driven self-serve guidance, not human-to-human matching (Phase 1-3).

## Users
- **School/UG Students**: Seeking direction and skill building.
- **Career Switchers**: Needing to map transferable skills to new fields.
- **MBA Students**: Pivoting to tech/analytics roles.
- **Non-Tech Students**: Entering tech fields from Arts/Commerce backgrounds.

## Constraints
- **Zero-Cost First**: All technology choices prioritize free tiers (Gemini Flash, Supabase, Vercel).
- **India-Contextual**: Must handle Indian academic backgrounds (B.Com, BBA) and market trends.
- **Mobile-First**: Indian students primarily use mobile devices (PWA prioritized).

## Success Criteria
- [ ] 85%+ users report clarity post-onboarding.
- [ ] Users see 25+ point improvement in Career Readiness Score within 90 days.
- [ ] AI recommendations rated relevant by 80%+ users.
